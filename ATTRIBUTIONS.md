`gorilla` is an original synthesized sound made for this project (synthesized with ffmpeg).
Everything else comes from Wikimedia Commons. The bundled `sounds/*.mp3` files are the
originals (in `raw/`) after **trimming (about 2 seconds) and loudness normalization**, which
counts as "modified" for the CC BY-SA sounds. The license of each original is listed below.
